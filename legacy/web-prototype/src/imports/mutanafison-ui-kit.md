Design a complete modern mobile UI for an Islamic productivity and accountability app called **Mutanafison**.

Mutanafison means "those who compete in good deeds". The purpose of the app is to allow two or more friends to compete in daily acts of worship such as the five daily prayers and Quran reading. The app should feel motivating, peaceful, and spiritually uplifting rather than competitive in an aggressive way.

The design should follow a **modern iOS style similar to Apple Health, Duolingo streak systems, and minimal habit tracking apps**. The interface must be extremely clean, calm, and easy to use daily.

Target device: **iPhone 15 Pro screen size**

Design language:

* minimalistic
* calm Islamic aesthetic
* modern typography
* smooth rounded cards
* soft gradients
* no clutter
* large tap targets
* friendly and motivational tone

Color palette:
Primary color: Emerald green (#0E7C61) representing growth and spirituality
Secondary color: Soft gold (#D4AF37) representing reward and achievement
Background: Very light warm gray (#F7F8F7)
Card background: White
Accent colors:

* Success green (#22C55E)
* Pending gray (#CBD5E1)

Typography:
Use a modern sans serif similar to **SF Pro / Inter**.

Use large headings and clear hierarchy.

The app icon should be a **crescent moon combined with a progress circle**, symbolizing spiritual progress.

Design the following screens:

1. ONBOARDING SCREEN

A welcoming screen explaining the concept.

Title:
"Compete in Good Deeds"

Subtitle:
"Stay consistent in prayer and Quran by competing with your friends."

Illustration:
Two friendly avatars progressing along a spiritual progress path.

Primary button:
"Start Competing"

Secondary button:
"Learn More"

The tone should feel positive and encouraging.

List the five prayers as cards:

Fajr
Dhuhr

Friend status

Use green checkmarks for completed prayers.

When the user taps a prayer card it toggles to completed with a small satisfying animation.
